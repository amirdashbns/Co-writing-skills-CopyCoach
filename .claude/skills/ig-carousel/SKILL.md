## The Hourglass Flow (Apply to EVERY Carousel)

Every carousel follows this shape regardless of formula:

```
Slide 1    → ZOOMED OUT: Big picture hook. Grab attention. 5-10 words max.
Slide 2-3  → ZOOM IN: Add context, set up the topic. Keep short and punchy.
Slide 4-7  → DEEP ZOOM: The meat. Examples, tips, stories, proof.
Slide 8-9  → ZOOM BACK OUT: Big takeaway. Should work as a standalone screenshot.
Slide 10   → CONNECT TO YOU: CTA that ties the topic back to the business.
```

### Momentum Rules
- Slides 1-3 are EVERYTHING. Short, punchy, build momentum. No walls of text early.
- Headings should work standalone — someone should get the message reading only headings.
- Plant seeds of curiosity between slides: "Here's what I mean…", "But here's the thing…", "It gets worse…"
- Use arrows (→) to visually pull people to the next slide.

### Readability Rules
- Left-align text (English)
- Headings: Big, bold, scannable
- Body text: 3 lines MAX per slide
- Use heading + subheading + body text hierarchy
- Don't say in 5 words what you can say in 1
- White space is your friend — leave margins

## Caption Guidelines

### DO:
- Add NEW information not in the carousel
- Tell a story that emphasizes the carousel's point
- Use the caption to go deeper on one slide's topic
- End with a question or engagement prompt
- Include 3-5 relevant hashtags (mix of popular + niche)
- Use keywords related to your business 3-4 times for SEO

### DON'T:
- Repeat the carousel title as the caption hook
- Restate the same information from the carousel
- Write a wall of text with no line breaks
- Use generic captions that could go on any post

### Caption Structure:
```
[HOOK — different angle than carousel title]

[STORY or ADDITIONAL CONTEXT — 2-4 short paragraphs]

[ENGAGEMENT PROMPT or CTA]

[HASHTAGS — 3-5 max]
```

## Voice Reminders (From Voice DNA)

When writing carousel copy, remember:
- **Tone:** Casual, direct, funny. Like talking to a friend at a coffee shop.
- **Sentences:** Short. Punchy. Fragments are common. Then a longer one to change rhythm.
- **Humor:** Dry, self-deprecating, cheeky. Never trying too hard.
- **Confidence:** Strong opinions delivered casually. No hedging.
- **Transitions:** "Here's the deal:", "But here's the thing:", "Now…", "Listen…"
- **Emphasis:** ALL CAPS for key words. Bold for important concepts.
- **Parentheticals:** Frequent. Used for humor and bonus commentary.
- **NEVER say:** "In today's rapidly evolving landscape," "game-changer," "unlock your potential," "let's dive in," "Here's the thing, though" or any hollow motivational language.

## ICP Pain Points to Tap Into

Use these as carousel hooks and angles:
- **The Editing Death Spiral:** "You generate with AI, then spend 45 min editing it to not sound like AI."
- **The Sameness Problem:** "Your AI content sounds like everyone else's AI content."
- **The Prompt Lottery:** "Sometimes AI gives you gold. Sometimes garbage. No consistency."
- **The Voice Gap:** "It might be well-written. But it doesn't sound like YOU."
- **The Context Amnesia:** "Every new chat, you re-explain who you are. Like training a new intern daily."
- **The Blank Prompt Box:** "You open ChatGPT, stare at the empty chat, and think 'what do I even say?'"

## Slide Image Delivery

When generating carousel slide images, ALWAYS follow these steps at the end:

1. **Generate slides** as PNG files at 1080×1350px
2. **Save to** `/knowledge/drafts/ig-carousel-{short-topic-slug}/`
3. **Commit and push** the images to the current branch
4. **Provide direct download links** for every slide using this raw GitHub URL format:

```
https://raw.githubusercontent.com/{owner}/{repo}/{branch}/knowledge/drafts/ig-carousel-{slug}/slide_XX.png
```

5. **List every link** in a numbered list so the user can click and save without navigating GitHub

This is the FINAL step of every carousel delivery. Do not skip it. Do not wait for the user to ask.

## Post-Creation Checklist

Before delivering the carousel, verify:
- [ ] Hook creates curiosity (would you stop scrolling?)
- [ ] Follows the hourglass flow (zoomed out → zoomed in → zoomed out → connect to you)
- [ ] Slides 1-3 are short and build momentum
- [ ] Headings work standalone (someone gets the message reading only headings)
- [ ] Seeds of curiosity between slides (arrows, "here's what I mean…", etc.)
- [ ] Big takeaway slide works as a standalone screenshot/share
- [ ] Voice matches Voice DNA (casual, direct, funny, no corporate speak)
- [ ] Content targets a specific ICP pain point
- [ ] Caption adds to the carousel (doesn't repeat it)
- [ ] CTA connects the topic back to YOU/your offer
- [ ] Body text is 3 lines max per slide
- [ ] No walls of text on any slide
- [ ] Not overly promotional (unless using a Sell formula intentionally)
- [ ] Direct download links provided for all slide images
