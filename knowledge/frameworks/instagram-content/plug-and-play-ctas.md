# Plug-and-Play CTAs

A CTA is a simple, clear, **specific** instruction about what to do next. Use one at the end of *every* caption (and on Slide 10 of carousels, in reels, and stories).

**Make it sound natural, not salesy:** connect it to the message with a **lead-in**. Most posts = 1 lead-in + 1 primary CTA. Add a secondary CTA only to boost engagement or remind about an offer (place secondary lead-ins *after* the primary CTA).

**Voice override:** rewrite all of these in the user's voice (`/context/voice-dna.json`).

---

## Lead-ins (transition into the CTA)

**"Want…?"** — "Want to learn more about [topic]?" / "Want the FULL [recipe/strategy]?" / "Want to take [undesirable task] off your hands?" / "And if you never want to [undesirable task] again?"

**"Ready…?"** — "Ready to say goodbye to [negative experience]?" / "Ready to say hello to [positive outcome]?" / "Ready to start [taking action]?" / "Ready to stop [negative behavior]?"

**"So here's… / So let's…"** — "So here's your [first step/invitation]." / "So let's [go after results] together." / "So here's what to do next time [you have this experience]."

**"Sound like…?"** — "Sound like just the thing you've been waiting for?" / "Sound like you?" / "Sound familiar?"

**"Need…?"** — "Need to [undesirable task] but don't know where to begin?" / "And if you need it all [done for you]?" / "And if you need [result] ASAP?"

**"And if you're thinking…"** — "I'm totally new to [topic]. Where do I begin?" / "How can I [get these results] too?" / "What if I [objection]?" / "Is there an easier way?" / "Can you take this off my hands?"

**Teasers / transitions:** "Turn your [before] into [after]." / "Let's make it happen." / "So why wait?" / "Don't worry. I've got you covered." / "P.S." / "BTW —" / "One last thing…" / "ICYMI:"

## CTAs by goal

### 1-to-1 Service
- Schedule: "Let's get you on my calendar!" / "You can schedule a discovery call at the link in my bio." / "Book a [session] with me!"
- DMs: "Send us a DM and tell us [what you need]!" / "Our DMs are open! See you there."
- Inquire/apply: "Fill out a quick inquiry form on my website!" / "Apply to work with me at the link in my bio."
- Full pitch: "You need [service]. In [time], you'll [action] and I'll [deliverable]. [How to sign up]." / "That's exactly what we'll do during [service]. You'll get [3-5 deliverables]. It's everything you need to [benefit] — even with [limitation]."

### Product Shop
- "Shop all [products] at the link in my bio!" / "See the whole collection at…" / "Find your perfect fit at…" / "Check out my full shop of [products]."

### Course / Masterclass
- "Sign up for [Course]! [Comment XYZ / Link in bio.]" / "That's exactly what [Course] is for. Sign up now at the link in my bio." / "You need [Course]. It's a [comprehensive] [course] for [audience] who want to [benefit]. Inside, you'll learn… [list]. Sign up now!" / "I teach all about this in my signature course, [Course]! For $X, you'll learn… [list]."

### Resource / Ebook / Template
- "Get [Resource], available now [where]." / "[Resource] has got you covered. Grab it at [where]." / "[Resource] is for any [audience] who want to [outcome]. Find it at [where]." / "And that's exactly why I created [Resource] — a [guide/template] for [audience] ready to [benefit]. AND it's only $X."

### Event / Workshop
- "Join me on [Date] for [Event]! [How to sign up]." / "I'm hosting a [workshop] on [Date] where I'll teach you [3 things]. [Only X spots left] — sign up at the link in my bio!" / "There's still time to get your tickets to [Event]!"

### Instagram Live
- "Want to learn more about [topic]? Join my Instagram Live!" / "Have questions? I'm going LIVE [date, time] with the As to all your Qs!"

### Freebie / Email List
- "Download [Freebie] for FREE now at [where]!" / "Snag [Freebie] ABSOLUTELY FREE when you [join my email list]." / "You know what's even cooler than me on IG? Me in your inbox." / "Join over [X] people chillin' with me in their inbox."

### Take the Quiz
- "Take the quiz: [Quiz Name]. Find it at [where]!" / "Are you an X or a Y? Only one way to find out — take the [Quiz] quiz!"

### Program Open (+ urgency variants)
- Open: "Enrollment is NOW OPEN to [program]! Snag your spot now!" / "DOORS ARE NOW OPEN for [program]."
- Limited spots: "Spots in [program] are filling up FAST!" / "Only X spots left!"
- Doors close soon: "Only X days left before doors close!" / "Doors close [Date]. You in?"
- Doors close today: "It's now or never! Join [program] before doors close at [time + timezone]." / "LAST CHANCE! Doors close TODAY."

### Discounts
- Auto-applied: "Get $X/X% off [products] from now until [date]! Discount applied at checkout."
- Code: "Use the code XYZ for X%/$X off [products]! Offer ends [date]."
- Ends soon/today: "Only X days left to get X% OFF." / "SALE ENDS TODAY! Get X% off if you buy before [time, timezone]." / "FINAL HOURS!"

### Increase Engagement (comments)
- With automation: "Comment [KEYWORD] below if you want to learn more." / "Comment [KEYWORD] and I'll send you a link." / "Drop a [EMOJI] if you want to try this."
- Clarifying Qs: "Drop your questions about [topic] in the comments!" / "What else do you want to learn about [topic]?"
- Connection Qs: "Who else loves [topic]?!" / "Are you struggling with [problem] too? Share in the comments." / "P.S. My fav [X] is [pick]. What's yours?"
- Prompting Qs: "Which one is your favorite?" / "How would you do this differently?" / "Which [topic] should I [action] next?"

### Increase Likes / Follows / Shares / Saves
- "SCROLL to the end to see [teaser]." / "Like, save, and share if you wanna see more like this!" / "FOLLOW for more." / "SAVE THIS POST for the next time you need [X]." / "SHARE this with someone who [needs it]." / "Tag a friend who needs to hear this!"

**Start:** Start, Begin, Try out, Get started, Kick off, Take the first step, Launch, Jump-start, "Give it a whirl"
**Join:** Sign up, Book, Schedule, Apply, Save your spot, Subscribe, Tune in to, "Hop on board," "Get in on"
**Contact:** Reach out, Get in touch with, Shoot (a DM), Inquire, Reply, Comment, Say hi, Hit us up, "Drop a DM"
**See more:** Explore, Check out, Scroll, Browse, View
