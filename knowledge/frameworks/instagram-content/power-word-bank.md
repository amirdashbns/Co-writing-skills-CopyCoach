# Power Word Bank

Words and phrases that add punch, organized by the emotion they trigger. Use them in post titles, headings, and CTAs to make copy more compelling.

**Voice override + caution:** layer these in only where they fit the user's voice (`/context/voice-dna.json`). Note that some users' voice DNA explicitly *bans* certain hype words (e.g. "game-changer," "unlock," "revolutionary"). Always check `never_say` before using.

## Phrases by emotion

**EASE:** "It's easier than you think." · "It's a no-brainer." · "This will save you HOURS." · "It's never been easier to…" · "Yes, it's that easy." · "This is your cheat sheet." · "Good news: it doesn't have to be this hard." · "Let's break it down." · "Think of it like this."

**EXCELLENCE:** "This is a game changer." · "You won't find anything better than this." · "This is the holy grail of ___." · "There is nothing else on the market quite like this." · "This is the ONLY ___ on the market." · "It's everything you need and more." · "It doesn't get better than this." · "Look no further! This is EXACTLY what you need."

**VALUE:** "But wait! There's more!" · "And that's just the beginning." · "And this week only…" · "And if you buy before {date}, you'll get…" · "Hurry! Sale ends soon!" · "Only X days left!" · "Last chance! Get it before it's gone!" · "It'll be so worth it."

**CURIOSITY:** "You're not gonna believe this." · "This is going to blow your mind." · "But wait… it gets better." · "But that's when everything changed." · "Plot twist!" · "You won't believe what happened next." · "I can't keep this secret any longer!" · "And here's the most exciting part…" · "It's not what you think."

**TRUST:** "Trusted by {job title} around the world." · "Backed by X years of research." · "And X happy clients agree." · "And we've got the data to prove it." · "But don't just take it from us. Take it from over X clients." · "100% satisfaction guaranteed." · "…or your money back."
