# Clickable Post Title Formulas

Title formulas for **Slide 1** of a carousel (also work for reels, static posts, captions). Organized by content bucket, but mix and match freely — a "Problem Awareness" title can make a great "Expert Showcase" post.

## Rules for clickable titles
- **5-7 words if you can; under 10 always.** Short and clear beats clever.
- **Give enough context** that someone scanning your feed instantly knows what you offer. Not "I wish I'd known this sooner…" but "The investment hack I wish I'd known sooner."
- **Pique interest:** challenge a belief, surprise with a fact/stat, reveal a truth, or use a story hook.
- **Use these as idea-starters, not restrictions.** Adjust the wording until it *sounds* like the user's voice (see `/context/voice-dna.json`). Layer in power words (see `power-word-bank.md`).
- **Format `[brackets]` = fill-in slots.** Each pattern below has one example.

## PROBLEM AWARENESS
- `Should you [take this action]?` → "Should you post on Instagram every day?"
- `Should you [take action] when [poor result]?` → "Should you post more when engagement is low?"
- `"[Common advice you disagree with]."` → "You don't need a website."
- `[Common advice] is [overrated/a waste of time/etc.].` → "Paying for marketing is unnecessary."
- `[Controversial statement/critique]` → "Reels are dead."
- `Do I/we/[audience] (really) need [product/action]?` → "Do I really need a sales page?"
- `[Can/Does/Do] [solution] really [make a difference]?` → "Can fresh paint really transform a home?"
- `[Is X effective / Does X work / Will X get results?]` → "Is SPF 30 strong enough?"
- `Is/Are [topic/product] [the/your/a] problem?` → "Is fast fashion the problem?"
- `Why [common advice/poor solution] is the problem` → "Why productivity hacks are the problem"
- `Why [action/product] [could be] [having negative effect]` → "Why caffeine might make you tired"
- `Why [action] isn't [working/enough]` → "Why washing your face isn't enough"
- `[Why/This is why] [action] [won't] [achieve result]` → "Why dieting won't help you lose weight"
- `If [deliverable/action] isn't working, here's why →` → "If your website isn't converting, here's why →"
- `Is [common action] [having a negative impact]?` → "Is calorie tracking ruining your social life?"
- `Can [action] [get a surprisingly better result]?` → "Can eating chocolate help you lose weight?"
- `[Are you/Does your] [negative experience]? Read this. →` → "Do you bloat after every meal? Read this"
- `Stop [taking common action]. Here's why. →` → "Stop stretching before your workout. Here's why"
- `Stop waiting to [take action]` → "Stop waiting to open a Roth IRA"
- `The #1 reason [negative result happens]` → "The #1 reason you don't get rave reviews"
- `The most common [cause of] [negative result]` → "The most common reason for inconsistent sales"
- `You don't need to [take action]` → "You don't need to grow your audience"
