# Business Profile - AI Copy Coach

## Business Name
AI Copy Coach

## What We Sell
A custom AI co-writing system installed directly into the client's workflow using Claude Code.
It captures their unique voice, writing style, and business context - so they can produce
weeks of content in hours without hiring a copywriter or sounding like AI.

## Price
$997 (launch/beta price)

## Delivery Process
1. Onboarding form - voice samples, business context, audience info, brand guidelines
2. System build - Voice DNA, Business Profile, ICP, custom skills calibrated to their voice
3. Walkthrough call - how to use the system, live demo, Q&A
4. 30-day support - async support for tweaks, recalibration, questions

## Core Differentiation
1. It is a system, not a service - You own it. No monthly fees. No dependency. Pay once, use forever.
2. Context engineering, not prompt engineering - The system deeply understands your voice, audience, and business.
3. It sounds like YOU, not like AI - Voice DNA captures your real patterns, quirks, and personality.

## Target Audience
Online service providers ($200K-$1M+/year) - coaches, consultants, agency owners, course creators
who are already using AI for content but frustrated with generic, robotic output.
They value their voice and brand, post regularly, and would rather invest money than time.

## Content Pillars
1. AI Writing Done Right (30%) - Authority content. How to use AI without sounding like AI.
2. The Content System (25%) - Proof content. Behind-the-scenes. Before/after examples.
3. Copywriting Principles That AI Makes Easier (20%) - Value content. Real copywriting concepts.
4. The Anti-Guru (15%) - Personality content. Hot takes. Calling out AI hype.
5. Building in Public (10%) - Relatability content. Sharing the journey.
